Come and see the history in your own backyard — at the Alameda Naval Air Museum in Alameda, California.

Our museum is home to a unique collection of artifacts and exhibits emphasizing the role of the Alameda Naval Air Station in the defense of our nation and as an integral part of the local community for over 50 years.  We are open on Saturdays and Sundays from 10:00am to 4:00pm.  Call us at (510) 522-4262 for more information
